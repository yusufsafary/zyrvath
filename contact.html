Whether you are a player with a question, a journalist working on a story, or someone who wants to collaborate, we read every message we receive.

General Inquiries

For anything that does not fit neatly into the categories below, reach us at hello@zyrvath.com. We aim to respond within a few business days.

Press and Media

If you are writing about our games or covering the studio, we are happy to provide assets, quotes, and interviews. Send press inquiries to press@zyrvath.com and include your publication and deadline.

Business and Partnerships

For partnership proposals, licensing, or business development conversations, write to business@zyrvath.com. Please include context about your proposal so we can give your message the attention it deserves.

Player Support

Running into a bug or need help with one of our games? Send details about the issue including your platform and what happened to support@zyrvath.com. The more context you include, the faster we can help.
