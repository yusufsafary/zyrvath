Zyrvath

A competitive ranked game built around warship selection, positioning, and reading your opponent. Not a casual experience. Not designed for everyone.

You pick a warship from one of six tiers. Your ship has four stats: hull integrity (HP), attack power, shield defense, and evasion. So does your opponent's. The match resolves around those eight numbers and the decisions you make with them.

There is a ranked ladder with eleven ranks and an ELO system. Your rating reflects your match outcomes. No shortcuts. No paid boosts.

The Fleet

Six tiers. Eighteen warships.

Each tier represents a distinct combat philosophy. Higher tier means more raw power and hull — and less forgiveness for bad positioning.

Tier 1

Scout Class

Built for speed and evasion. Lowest HP in the fleet. High dodge chance. Hit and run or die trying.
