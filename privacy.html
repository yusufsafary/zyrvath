Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Zyrvath Studio ("we", "our") operates zyrvath.com. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we handle it. The short version: we collect almost nothing.

What we collect

When you visit zyrvath.com, our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may automatically log standard server data: your IP address, browser type, referring URL, and pages visited. We do not control this logging and do not access it for marketing purposes.

If you contact us by email, we receive the information you choose to include in your message.

The game demo may store gameplay data (ship selections, match outcomes, rank ratings) in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

What we do not collect

  • We do not run advertising networks or sell data to third parties.
  • We do not use tracking pixels or fingerprinting.
  • We do not require account registration to use this site or the demo.

Third-party services

This site uses Google Fonts, which loads font files from Google's servers. Google may collect usage data per their own privacy policy. We load fonts over a direct connection and do not pass any user data to Google beyond what a standard browser request includes.

Cookies

This site does not set first-party cookies. See our Cookie Policy for details.

Data retention

Email correspondence is retained only as long as necessary to respond to your message. Server logs maintained by our hosting provider are subject to their own retention policies.

Your rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at studio@zyrvath.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.
