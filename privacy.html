Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2025

Who We Are

Zyrvath operates the website at zyrvath.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our site, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

Information you give us directly

If you contact us by email or through any form on this site, we receive the information you choose to include in that message. This typically includes your name and email address.

Information collected automatically

When you visit zyrvath.com, basic technical information is collected automatically by our hosting provider. This includes your IP address, browser type, the pages you visit, and the time of your visit. This information is used to keep the site running and to understand general usage patterns.

Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies to make the site work properly and to understand how visitors use it. You can read more about this in our Cookie Policy.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to messages and inquiries you send us
  • To maintain and improve the website
  • To understand how our site is being used in aggregate
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your information for advertising purposes.

Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this policy. Email correspondence is typically retained for up to two years unless there is a specific reason to keep it longer.

Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete the information we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@zyrvath.com.

Third Party Services

Our site uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. When your browser loads a Google Font, Google receives your IP address. You can review Google's privacy practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review it periodically.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@zyrvath.com.
