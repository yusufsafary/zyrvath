What we stand for

Atmosphere over spectacle

A still room can be more tense than an explosion. We design for feeling first and feature lists never.

Respect the player

No hand-holding, no padding. If something is in the game, it earns its place.

Ship when it is ready

Calendars do not make great games. We release when we believe in what we are releasing.

Small is a strength

Every person on the team ships work that matters. No committee, no compromise on vision.

Depth over volume

One memorable game is worth more than ten forgettable ones. We choose depth every time.

Sound is design

Audio is not decoration. It carries information, builds tension, and defines worlds as much as visuals do.
