About the Studio

We build games people think about long after they stop playing.

Zyrvath is an independent game studio. We are a small team driven by one conviction: games are the most powerful creative medium on the planet, and they deserve to be treated that way.

Who We Are

Zyrvath was founded by developers and designers who had spent years building games for other people. At some point, the conversation shifted. We started asking what we would make if nobody was telling us what to make. The answer became Zyrvath.

We do not chase trends. We chase the feeling you get when a game surprises you in a way that feels completely inevitable in hindsight.

How We Work

Small by design. Every person on the team ships work that matters. We prefer depth over breadth, polish over volume, and a game that knows exactly what it is over one that tries to be everything at once.

We build slowly and deliberately. When we release something, it is because we believe in it, not because a calendar told us to.

What We Believe

  • Games should respect the player's time and intelligence.
  • Atmosphere is not decoration. It is part of the design.
  • A great game loop is worth more than a great trailer.
  • We would rather make one thing memorable than ten things forgettable.
