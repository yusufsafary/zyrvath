What We're Building

The Game

Zyrvath is a competitive space combat game. 18 warships. Six tiers. An ELO-based ranked system. The game is built around ship selection, positioning, and reading your opponent before they read you.

It is not an MMO. It is not an idle game. It is not a battle royale. It is a focused competitive experience designed to be played for years, not hours.

The Setting

The Astral Empire. Three thousand years of galactic order. Then Zyrvath — a dead star system with no strategic value — was found. Warlords came. Conflict followed. No one agreed on why. No one left.

The setting is a backdrop, not a tutorial. The game does not hold your hand and neither does the lore.

Principles

Independence means something

We have no publisher and no investor deadline. That is a choice, not a circumstance. It means we can wait until the thing is actually good.

Respect the player's time

No daily login bonuses. No artificial gates. No content designed to maximize session length. You play when you want, for as long as you want.

The demo is not a pitch

The demo is the game in its current state. If it is rough in places, that is honest. Play it to evaluate it, not to be impressed by it.

Est. 2024

Questions? We read every message.

No automated responses. No support ticket system. Just a real reply from the people building the game.
