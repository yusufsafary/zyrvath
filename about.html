About the Studio

We do not make games for everyone.

We make games for the players who want to feel something they cannot quite name. That is a smaller audience. We are fine with that.

Origin

Zyrvath was built by people who spent years making games for other studios. We made things we were proud of, but we always went home thinking about the game we actually wanted to make. Eventually we stopped waiting for permission.

How We Operate

Small, intentional, and slow on purpose. Every decision on the team is made by someone who also ships the work. No layers. No committees. When you play something from us, you are playing exactly what we intended.

What We Prioritize

  • Atmosphere over volume. Feeling over feature count.
  • Games that respect the player's intelligence.
  • Silence as a design tool, not a missing asset.
  • Shipping when it is ready, not when a deadline says so.

Contact

We welcome messages from players, press, and potential collaborators. The best way to reach us is through our contact page.
