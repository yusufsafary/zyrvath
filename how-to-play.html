How to Play

Four stats. Your move.

Zyrvath does not have a tutorial. This page is the tutorial. Read it once. The rest comes from playing.

Ship Statistics

Every ship has four numbers.

Understand what each one does. The outcome of every match is decided by these stats and how you leverage them.

HP
Hull Integrity. Total damage a ship can absorb before destruction. Higher HP means more resilience, but it does not guarantee survival — a ship with low shield defense bleeds HP fast regardless of its total.
ATK
Attack Power. Base damage dealt per round of combat. This number hits your opponent's shield first. Whatever gets through hits their hull. Raw attack without strategy still wins matches. Raw attack with strategy wins decisively.
SHD
Shield Defense. Damage absorption rating. Incoming attack is reduced by the shield value before it reaches the hull. A ship with 80 ATK against a ship with 30 SHD deals 50 effective damage per round. Do not ignore this number.
EVA
Evasion. Percentage chance to completely dodge an incoming attack. At high evasion ratings, a ship can survive engagements it has no business surviving. At low evasion, every hit lands. Higher tiers generally sacrifice evasion for raw power.
Combat Calculation

Effective damage per round = Attacker ATK − Defender SHD (minimum 0). If the defender's evasion check succeeds, the round deals zero damage. Hull integrity decreases by effective damage each round until it reaches zero.

Ship Selection

Pick your tier. Know the tradeoff.

No tier is strictly superior. A Tier 1 Scout with high evasion can defeat a Tier 6 Sovereign if the dice go right. But variance decreases at higher tiers — dreadnoughts are slow and relentless. Scouts are fast and fragile.

Tier 1–2

Scout / Interceptor

Best evasion in the fleet. Lowest HP. You win by dodging more than you get hit. Against Sovereign-class ships, evasion is your only path to victory.

Best against: Ships with low attack.
Weak to: High sustained damage output.

Tier 3

Cruiser

The most forgiving tier. Balanced stats mean you are never badly outmatched and never dominant. Good for learning the game. Good for consistent ranked performance.

Best against: Everything, competently piloted.
Weak to: Tier 6 raw damage.

Tier 4–6

Destroyer / Sovereign

Maximum firepower and hull. Near-zero evasion means every hit lands. You are not trying to dodge. You are trying to outlast and outgun. Works until it does not.

Best against: Low-evasion targets.
Weak to: Evasive Scouts getting lucky.

Controls

The interface is minimal. Deliberately.

Click / Tap

Select a ship from the roster. Clicking reveals stats.

Select

Confirm ship selection and lock in your choice.

Battle

Initiate the match. Combat resolves automatically based on stats.

Rematch

Requeue immediately. Your opponent may switch ships. So can you.

Leaderboard

View the current ranked standings. Updated in real time.

One Rule

Read the stats before you commit. You cannot change your ship once the match starts. The one that looks invincible on paper might be countered by exactly what your opponent picked.
