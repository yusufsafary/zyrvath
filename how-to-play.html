Guide

How to play our games.

Our games do not hold your hand. That is intentional. Here is what you need to get started.

The Core Approach

Everything in our games is placed intentionally. If something stands out, pay attention to it. If something is quiet, that is also intentional. The environment is always speaking. The game never gives you more information than you are meant to have at any given moment.

Controls

Keyboard

  • Move: WASD or arrow keys
  • Interact: E or Space
  • Pause: Escape
  • All controls are remappable in settings

Controller

Full gamepad support. We test on controller. It works the same as keyboard, sometimes better.

Accessibility

  • Adjustable text size and contrast
  • Colorblind modes
  • Fully remappable controls
  • Game speed adjustment in pacing-sensitive sections

If you have a need we have not covered, write to support@zyrvath.com.

Tips

  • You cannot fail by going slowly. Take the time.
  • Audio is information. Use headphones if you can.
  • Stuck? Try the last thing you did, but differently.
  • The game autosaves. Manual saves are also available at any time.
